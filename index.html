SchengenTrips

Worry-free EU Travel

SchengenTrips takes the stress out of planning multiple trips to the EU Schengen by doing the visa-free calculations for you.

Features

  • Plan trips in the future or record trips in the past
  • View your remaining allowance at any point in time
  • Get early warnings if you plan to over stay
  • A handy widget to keep track of your current allowance
